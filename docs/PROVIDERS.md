## Planned Connectors

- **InfinityPay**: Direct Pix integration.
- **Inter**: Corporate Banking API.
- **BTG Pactual**: Treasury API.

## Partner Program

Providers who wish to become "Recommended Connectors" in the FlowPay ecosystem can apply for the **Verified Partner** tier, which includes:
- Native integration in the core repository.
- Joint marketing to commercial partners.
- Optimized settlement routing.
